SpeedTimer
Bored of using other Cubing Timers with their appaling design and lack of simplicity? Me too. Instead of waiting for a new timer to come out, I decided to create my own free timer to combat this. And as a Cuber Myself, I won't let you down. If you're Interested, visitspeedtimer.xyzright now!
Timing
Other Timers are really complicated. Let's face it, sometimes you dont know what does what. SpeedTimer is designed to improve on this, capatilising on Simplicity and is easy to understand for people who are just starting to cube as well. Just press the spacebar, and voila! The stats are also very well places so you can see how to improve, without confusing yourself at the same time. Let's not forget about the easy way to Delete your accidental solves!
Sessions
When Keeping Track fo your Progress, Organisation is very Important. For This, I've Created a Simple Sessions System to Create and Monitor your Sessions, Be It over Different Cube Types, Different Time Periods, or any thing you want!
Algorithms
Ever wanted to keep all of your Algorithms in one place? SpeedTimer's got this too! With Algorithms Hand-Picked by a consistent sub 8 solver and sub 13 Algorithm Expert. If you're not happy with their algorithm, you can always change it, and rotate the case image as well. For you big cube solvers, big cube algs will come too! And Don't worry, all these changes will be saved! :)
Settings
Customisation is important to make you feel comfortable while Cubing. I've Added a Settings Page for you to customise SpeedTimer to your preferences, and also Manage Your Data, If you want to clear any Info, or Reset your Settings in The Case that you Want to Try something new!
