SpeedTimer

Bored of using other timers with their appaling design and lack of simplicity? Me too. Instead of waiting for a new timer to come out, I decided to create my own free timer to combat this. And as a Cuber Myself, I won't let you down. Use The SpaceBar to start and stop the timer, and play around with settings and cube types. Happy Cubing!
